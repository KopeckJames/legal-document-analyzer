Upload Process

The document upload process in LegalDataInsights involves several automated steps:

  1. Document is securely uploaded to our system
  2. Text is extracted and processed
  3. AI analysis identifies key legal concepts
  4. Relevant statutes are automatically identified
  5. Document is indexed for later reference

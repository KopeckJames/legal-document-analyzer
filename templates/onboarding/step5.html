Sample Knowledge Entry
Confidentiality in Consulting Agreements

Standard confidentiality provisions in consulting agreements typically include reciprocal obligations that protect both parties' proprietary information. Key elements to consider include:

  • Definition of confidential information
  • Exclusions for public domain information
  • Duration of confidentiality obligation
  • Return or destruction of materials upon termination
